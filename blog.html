  • Always match your shoes with your belt for a polished look.
  • Layering is key in winter — mix textures like wool and cotton.
  • Accessorize minimally — a watch or bracelet is often enough.
  • White sneakers go with almost every casual outfit.
  • Invest in a few quality basics; they last longer and look better.
